BIO
INCUBATOR

CUSATECH Foundation established RISE Bioincubator in 2021 with the support from Rashtriya Uchchattar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA), Govt. of India., to strengthen and empower the entrepreneurial potential of emerging Life Sciences startups from CUSAT Students, alumni and others to undertake research and innovation for translating innovations into commercial products and technologies in the areas of biotechnology (biopharma, agrobiotech, industrial biotech, clean technology), healthcare, marine sciences, biomass value addition/ renewable fuels/chemicals/materials, bioinformatics, and related disciplines.


INCUBATOR

We provide our incubator’s innovator/startup companies with dedicated and shared incubation laboratory spaces with access to lab equipment, amenities and general services in order to minimize capital liabilities on innovator/startup companies and their by transforming viable idea to viable prototype, product/ technology or service and ready to offer it to market in a sustainable way.
